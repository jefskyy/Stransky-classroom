Scenario 1 of 6

The First Law asks whether energy quantities balance. The Second Law asks whether the proposed direction and conversion of energy are possible. Heat can be moved from a colder region to a hotter region when work is supplied. A cyclic heat engine cannot receive heat from only one reservoir and convert all of it to net work. In each case, first read the arrows, then verify the energy balance, identify the governing Second-Law statement, and classify the device.
